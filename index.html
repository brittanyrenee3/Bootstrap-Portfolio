About Me

​

My name is Brittany-Renee Davis. I am a digital marketer and content creator for a fashion line based in Los Angeles with showrooms around the nation. I also freelance as a content developer for multiple lines and fashion PR Firms located in the Arts District and the CMC.

I graduated from Tamkang University in Taipei, Taiwan with a degree in business communications and am currently studying computer coding. In my free time, I write for an environmental sustainability blog and volunteer with the Koreatown homeless advocacy non-profit, "Ktown for All."

By the end of this cohort, I hope to feel confident in implementing the tools I've learned to accomplish daily content tasks without using platforms such as Brandboom, Constant Contact or Squarespace.
